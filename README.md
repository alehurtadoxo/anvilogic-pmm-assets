anvilogic-pmm-assets
A centralized repository for Product Marketing Management (PMM) assets at Anvilogic, organized by funnel stage to support go-to-market initiatives, partner enablement, and customer engagement.

Overview
This repository maintains all marketing assets, messaging frameworks, and content deliverables created by the PMM team. Assets are organized by funnel stage (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU) to ensure clear alignment with buyer journey stages and enable easy discovery for sales, partnerships, and marketing teams.
Repository Structure
Branches
main

Purpose: Production-ready assets approved for external use

Contents:

Final versions of whitepapers, case studies, and sales collateral
Approved messaging frameworks and positioning documents
Partner co-marketing materials
Product launch assets
Storylane Demos


Access: Read-only for most teams; PMM manages updates

Branches:

tofu (Top of Funnel)

Purpose: Awareness-stage content for prospects discovering Anvilogic
Contents:

Blog posts and thought leadership articles
Social media content (LinkedIn, Twitter/X)
Industry reports and research summaries
Educational webinar materials
Infographics and data visualizations
SEO-optimized content
Competitive intelligence summaries (public-facing)


Target Audience: Security professionals, SOC leaders, CISOs researching solutions

mofu (Middle of Funnel)

Purpose: Consideration-stage content for engaged prospects evaluating solutions
Contents:

Solution briefs and technical overviews
Use case documentation
Product comparison guides
Webinar recordings and demo scripts
ROI calculators and TCO analyses
Partner solution briefs (Cribl, Torq, etc.)
Detection engineering showcases
Platform capability deep-dives


Target Audience: Evaluation teams, security architects, procurement stakeholders

bofu (Bottom of Funnel)

Purpose: Decision-stage content supporting final purchase decisions
Contents:

Customer case studies and testimonials
Implementation guides and success plans
Detailed pricing and packaging materials
Security and compliance documentation
POC/pilot frameworks
Sales battle cards
Competitive win/loss analysis (internal)
Executive summaries and business cases


Target Audience: Decision-makers, procurement, implementation teams

development

Purpose: Work-in-progress content and experimental messaging
Contents:

Draft assets under review
A/B testing variations
Messaging experiments
Template development


Access: PMM team only

TOFU_Blog_DataLakeConversion_v1_2025-01

BOFU_CaseStudy_FinancialServices_v1_2025-01

Usage Guidelines

Finding Assets: Browse by funnel stage branch to locate content appropriate for your prospect's buying stage
Version Control: Always use the latest version from main branch for external sharing
Customization: Copy assets to your local environment before customizing for specific opportunities
Feedback: Submit issues or PRs with suggested updates, messaging improvements, or new asset requests
Approval Process: All assets must be reviewed by PMM before merging to main
